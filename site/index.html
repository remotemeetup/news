Remote Meetup News #001 - may 28th 2016

How is it going with Remote Meetups?

It's been a while that you have logged in our Slack Channel. I would like to just to say how we are going and the latest updates!

Doing a Remote Meetup is a little bit difficult, because users report don't have the same feeling as a real meetup. For this reason, We were looking for some tools for helping us to do that. Here, a big thanks to Carlisia Campos and William Kennedy, GoBridge in general, people from our Go branch who have found a Nice tool: BigMarker.

BigMarker is a paid tool, we got a sponsorship from Hired.com which helped us to pay this Main Channel for Remote Meetup. Channel is here! Using this, the attendees interaction will be better. If you want, you can subscribe to this channel and get the latest meetups! We have a paid account up to 100 users.

Go Branch got a sponsorship from Compose.io, which helped them to pay their own channel. It means, all the Remote Meetups from Go will be centralised in a single channel and not the Main Channel. BTW, if you want to learn Go, it's time! A lot of awesome upcoming meetups in the next weeks!

Helping Meetups around the World

Another idea we got on-the-fly was helping meetups. We are partner of two Meetups around the world: ParisRb - the biggest meetup in Europe. Green Ruby - A cool meetup in Taipei, Taiwan! These meetups can use or channel and livestream their meetups.

Elm Remote Meetup

DailyDrip is planning to create a Elm Remote Meetup. We are also partner with DailyDrip. They will be using our Channel and using Remote Meetup to do that!
